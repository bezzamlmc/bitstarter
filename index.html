Are you tired of spams about buying and selling used items filling up your college email inbox? Do you want to deck out your college room? And what are you going to do with all your stuff when you graduate...

CollegeExchange can help you with these problems. With CollegeExchange, you can easily buy, sell, and auction items with other students from your college. There is no more hassle over shipping, knowing the seller or buyer, or annoying emails clogging your inbox. Through our website and app you will be able to post your used or unwanted items for sale and see other items on the market.

Anything from clothes, stationary, bikes, furniture, electronics, and food can be bought or sold on CollegeExchange. However CollegeExchange isn't just limited to buying and selling. Travel and dining tips can also be shared on CollegeExchange. Check out that hot, new restaurant that everyone is talking about. Or discover that hidden gem and share it with the rest of campus. CollegeExchange is perfect for freshman who are just moving into the dorms, seniors who need to sell their stuff before leaving, and the inner shopaholic and socialite in all of us!
